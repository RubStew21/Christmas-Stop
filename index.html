ğŸ¥› Homemade Eggnog

Ingredients:

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale
  2. Heat milk and cream until steaming
  3. Slowly temper egg mixture with hot milk
  4. Cook until thickened
  5. Add nutmeg and vanilla
  6. Chill and serve

â˜• Homemade Hot Chocolate

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Heat milk in saucepan
  2. Whisk in cocoa and sugar
  3. Add chocolate chips and stir until melted
  4. Add vanilla
  5. Serve hot with marshmallows
